About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Fudan University, specializing in Electrical Engineering. My research focuses deeply on digital IC front-end design, as well as the automated design and optimization of RISC-V CPUs. I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my expertise in microarchitecture design space exploration and hardware optimization.
